Full Stack Web Developer.

Daniel Canet is a Software Engineering Manager based in Barcelona. He is a graduate of the Facultat d'Informàtica de Barcelona (FIB), one of the top 10 computer science schools in Europe.

He is a software engineer with more than 15 year of experience. Passionate about the open-source movement, best practices, agile methodologies, design systems, devops and the internet. Enthusiastic about projects that have a positive impact on culture and local communities.

Spent his early professional years on a national company using propietary technologies from IBM and Microsoft. After which he founded his first web development shop, strabinarius, where he started to create experiences on the internet for remarkable brands. He then went on to work as an independent creative technologist and joined Xnographics, a multidisciplinary design team from Barcelona. After that, he joined Season, where he directed a bunch of designers, coders and marketers, while keep on producing code for back-end, front-end and deploy automation. More recently he founded facts&fictions, a creative studio to improve tools and methodologies while focusing on more local, personal and art based projects.

Currently Engineering Team Lead of Web Integrations at WIRIS in Barcelona. Previous collaborations include CASIO, BMW, Carrefour, Armand Basi/Lacoste, Hangar.org, Volkswagen, Amnesty International, Bodegas Martín Códax, CREAF and more.

He also does experimental things and bots with Javascript.
